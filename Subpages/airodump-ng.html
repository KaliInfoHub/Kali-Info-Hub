Airodump-ng

What is Airodump-ng?

Airodump-ng is part of the Aircrack-ng suite used for wireless network analysis and packet capture. It helps identify nearby access points, clients, and collect data needed for cracking WPA/WPA2 keys.

Basic Usage

Start monitoring on a wireless interface:

airodump-ng wlan0mon

Target a specific BSSID and channel:

airodump-ng --bssid 00:11:22:33:44:55 -c 6 -w capture wlan0mon

Practical Example
